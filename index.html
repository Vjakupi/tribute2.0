Keanu Reeves

The Best Modern Action Star

Here is a brief timeline of Keanu's life and accomplishments

  • 1964 - Born in Beirut, Lebanon
  • 1985 - Makes acting debut in television series called "Hanging In"
  • 1989 - Stars in "Bill and Ted's Excellent Adventure", his first commercial and critical success
  • 1991- Stars in "Bill and Ted's Bogus Journey", garnering further praise and attention
  • 1991 - Stars in "Point Break" opposite Patrick Swayze and is further noticed for more adult roles
  • 1995 - Stars in "Speed" opposite Sandra Bullock. The film is a critical and box office smash hit and garners two Academy Awards.
  • 1999 - Stars in "The Matrix"
  • 2001 - Endures tragedy when his wife is killed in a car crash
  • 2013 - Releases his directorial debut "The Man of Tai Chi"
  • 2014 - Stars in "John Wick", establishing the greatest action franchise of all time.
  • 2020 - Releases "Bill and Ted Face the Music"
  • 2020 - Ranked #4 on The New York Times "Greatest 25 Actors of the 21st Century"

"In addition to being humble and generous, Reeves is often in the news for his random acts of kindness, which range from giving up his seat on a crowded subway train to secretly donating huge sums of money to children's hospitals."

-- Libby Torres, Keanu Reeves reporter for Insider
